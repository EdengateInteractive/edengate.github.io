Project Crucible Devlog #1

April 30 2024

First devlog of our upcoming game is out.

The working title currently for the game is Project Crucible. We chose the name crucible for it's abstract definition, which states "a place or situation in which concentrated forces interact to cause or influence change or development". This definition fit very well due to the setting and events that happen within the game.
